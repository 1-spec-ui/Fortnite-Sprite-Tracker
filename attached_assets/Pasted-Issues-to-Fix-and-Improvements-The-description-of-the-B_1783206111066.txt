Variant Payout Modifiers

When you extract a special variant of a Sprite, a passive multiplier or flat bonus alters your
final Sprite Dust payout:

· Base Variant: No extra multiplier.

. Gummy Variant: Grants a 10% bonus to all Sprite Dust earned upon extraction (some early community reports note up to 20%). For example, a standard 500 dust extraction
becomes 550 to 600 Sprite Dust

. Gold / Galaxy / Gummy Variants: These yield the standard base rarity dust payouts upon extraction.
