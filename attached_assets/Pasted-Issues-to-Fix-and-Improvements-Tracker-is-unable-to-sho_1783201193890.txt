Sprite Dust Costs:
All base rare tier sprites cost 2,000 dust.
All rare tier sprite variants cost 4,000 dust.
All base epic tier sprites cost ~3,000~ dust.
All epic tier sprite variants cost 6,000 dust.
All base legendary tier sprites cost ~5,000~ dust. 
All legendary variants cost 10,000 dust.
All base mythic tier sprites cost ~7,500~ dust.
All mythic tier sprite variants cost 15,000 dust.

The presumed pattern is that sprite dust costs for variants is twice the amount of the base sprite. The ~ indicates costs that are assumed based on this pattern.


















Sprite Dust Services Terminal Costs:
You can also spend your currency at Sprite Dust Services terminals located at Extraction Sites across the map for various upgrades and perks:
Locate Sprites Map Marker: 100 Sprite Dust
20,000 Battle Pass XP: 1,000 Sprite Dust (Limit: Once per day)
Portable Extractor: 2,000 Sprite Dust (Limit: Once per day)
Weapon Rarity Upgrades: Costs scale by tier: 
Common to Uncommon: 250 Sprite Dust
Uncommon to Rare: 500 Sprite Dust
Rare to Epic: 1,000 Sprite Dust
Epic to Legendary: 2,000 Sprite Dust
